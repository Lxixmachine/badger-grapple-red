# BADGER GRAPPLE RED - THE VISION (v1.1 RATIFIED)

The founding document. Every manifesto, spec, system, and sprite hangs
under this. When any decision is unclear, settle it against §1.

## 1. The fantasy and the why

**You are a freshman walk-on at Wisconsin. You wake up in the Camp
Randall locker room on day one of the season; by spring you have built
the room around you and brought a national title home.**

This game is for the team and the recruits. Every decision serves two
feelings: "I know this place" (Madison, rendered with love — recognition
is the product) and "this is what a season feels like" (Pokémon's
comfort-loop carrying wrestling's soul). We do not reskin Pokémon; we
translate its spatial and emotional grammar into wrestling's native
language (CITY_DESIGN_MANIFESTO Part X is the translation dictionary).

## 2. Who you are

A freshman wrestler - NOT the coach. Coach is the Professor figure. On day one
you pick your **persona** - your style (Shooter / Rider / Scrambler) and its animal
spirit — and *you are your own starter*: your persona is roster slot #1
and wrestles in your duals. Rex is a returning teammate in your weight class;
the opening wrestle-off and his transfer make him the rival who stays ahead all
season.

## 4. The world (Season One region)

CAMP RANDALL (home town: locker room, wrestling room, Coach, captain)
-> R1 -> FIELD HOUSE town (★1, where the team really competes; X: the
Field House + Arch) -> R2 = STATE STREET -> CAPITOL SQUARE (★2; X: the
enterable Capitol) -> R3 Monona shore -> KOHL CENTER town (★3 = the
conference tournament) -> AIRPORT -> flight -> ST. LOUIS (NATIONALS;
X: the Gateway Arch). BASCOM HILL optional north branch off R2; the
western wilds (Lakeshore Path / Picnic Point) unchanged, hanging off
the Field House town. Region map: the boarded proposal (Tony-reviewed).
Geometry law: one plane, validator-checked, Town Map generated from it.

## 5. The loop

- **Minute**: walk a route, cross open mats, scout, recruit, get
  ambushed, manage the lineup.
- **Town**: arrive -> recover -> shop -> find the X-factor -> earn the
  badge -> a gate you remember opens.
- **Season (the story)**: preseason in the locker room -> duals ->
  conference at the Kohl -> the flight -> St. Louis -> walk back into
  Camp Randall and read the trophy case. The first area matters last.
  The existing day/period clock is the season calendar.

# Camp Randall Exterior Visual Contract

Status: authoritative for the v22.33 compact-town rebuild.

## Spatial Contract

- Exact 24x20 gameplay cells at 32 pixels per cell.
- Exact 15x10 camera; every review is judged at native 480x320.
- One two-cell south connection at columns 11-12, aligned to R1.
- Every building, threshold, prop, actor, and collision owner lands on the same grid.
- The only walkable cells under a structure are explicitly marked in its collision mask.

## Composition Contract

- The 11x5 stadium terminates the north-south limestone walk without filling a whole camera.
- The Team Building sits west and the Coach's Office sits east on offset branches.
- The memory garden and practice pond create two different relief spaces; they are not mirrored filler.
- Forest masses visibly close every map edge except the authored south route.
- Cardinal is reserved for building identity and accents. Paths stay pale and quiet.
- No 15x10 review may be mostly uninterrupted lawn or contain an isolated prop collage.

## Required Reviews

1. Stadium identity: facade, forecourt, and both approach lamps.
2. Home core: Team Building, authored threshold, branch walk, and quad activity.
3. Coach and relief: Coach's Office, pond, garden edge, and connected circulation.
4. South exit: dirt transition, visible forest closure, and the exact two-cell route seam.

The package closes only after collision reachability, story flow, native phone framing,
and a side-by-side FireRed composition review all pass.

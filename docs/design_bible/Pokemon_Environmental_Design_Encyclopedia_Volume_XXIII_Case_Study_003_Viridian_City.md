# 1. Scope

Viridian City is the player's first true hub.

Responsibilities include:

- expanding the player's mental model,
- introducing recurring city services,
- providing optional interaction,
- gating future progression.

---

# 2. Functional Role

## Observation

The city contains more destinations than Pallet Town while remaining easy to understand.

## Interpretation

Complexity increases gradually rather than abruptly.

## Transferable Lesson

Increase the number of choices before increasing navigational difficulty.

---

# 3. Urban Structure

Observed characteristics:

- Central circulation.
- Distinct public destinations.
- Residential support buildings.
- Open navigation corridors.
- Multiple exits with different gameplay significance.

Questions for future measurement:

- Average building spacing.
- Open-space ratio.
- Landmark visibility distance.

---

# 4. Navigation

The city teaches players to identify:

- major services,
- exits,
- optional interactions,
- progression blockers.

Navigation relies primarily on spatial organization rather than explicit instruction.

Supported Rules:

- GUIDE-001
- ARCH-001
- TOWN-001
- SCREEN-001

---

# 5. Service Placement

Core services should be:

- easy to locate,
- visually differentiated,
- connected by obvious circulation.

Interpretation:

Frequently visited buildings deserve superior placement rather than excessive decoration.

---

# 6. Progression Gating

Observation:

Not every exit is immediately usable.

Interpretation:

The city introduces the concept that the world can temporarily exceed the player's current capabilities.

Transferable Lesson:

Gates should motivate curiosity rather than create confusion.

---

# 9. Rule Validation

Strongly Supported

- Clear circulation
- Distinct destination hierarchy
- Readable progression
- Low visual clutter

Pending Measurement

- Building setbacks

- Density map

---

# 10. Transferable Lessons

For early-game cities:

- Organize before decorating.
- Make frequently used buildings easy to rediscover.
- Reserve visual emphasis for functional importance.
- Introduce progression gates without obscuring the intended route.
- Preserve generous navigation space.

# Camp Randall Exterior v2

Generated with ChatGPT image generation on 2026-07-10, then reduced with nearest-neighbor sampling to the final 448x288 game dimensions and a 48-color indexed palette.

Prompt direction: create an original top-down GBA-era campus map with a compact stadium landmark, two distinct supporting buildings, a cardinal brick path, readable lawns and courtyards, small flower and lamp accents, and a deep irregular tree boundary. Every building must read as one complete multi-tile drawing with an obvious entrance. Preserve open walking lanes and strong silhouettes at phone scale.

References used for visual analysis: the prior Camp Randall composition and user-supplied Pokemon FireRed/LeafGreen tileset and town screenshots. The references were used to study scale, projection, terrain transitions, density, and hierarchy only. No Nintendo pixels, buildings, or map layouts were copied or traced.

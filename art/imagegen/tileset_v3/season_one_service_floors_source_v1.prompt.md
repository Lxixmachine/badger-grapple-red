# Service Floors V1

Built with ChatGPT Image Generation as two original, quiet interior floor
swatches: a warm cream and mint-gray Trainer's Room floor, and a desaturated
blue-gray Bucky's Locker Room floor. Both were requested as fully opaque
square textures on a flat `#ff00ff` field with no objects, perspective,
lighting gradients, text, or copied assets.

The compiler reduces each source to a seamless 16x16 logical tile and its
declared three-color material ramp before exact 2x export.

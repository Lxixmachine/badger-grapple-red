# Lakeshore Path and Picnic Point landmark source

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: production source atlas for a 32px-grid top-down handheld RPG

Create one original GBA-era pixel-art sprite sheet containing six isolated
Lakeshore Path and Picnic Point route landmarks for an original Wisconsin
wrestling adventure game. The sheet will be mechanically cropped and reduced
into exact grid-native metatile stamps.

Use a perfectly flat solid `#ff00ff` chroma-key background, completely uniform
with no shadows, gradients, texture, floor, or lighting variation. Use polished
original late-GBA top-down 3/4 pixel art with crisp hard pixel clusters, compact
three-to-four-shade ramps per material, and dark tinted one-pixel outlines. Do
not use antialiasing, blur, painterly noise, or modern high-resolution rendering.

Arrange the following assets in three columns by two rows with generous equal
gutters. Keep each object isolated and fully visible inside its own rectangular
footprint.

Top row:

1. A five-cell-wide by four-cell-tall wooden Lake Mendota pier viewed from
   above, with a pale limestone landing at its south end and the dock extending
   north.
2. A four-cell-wide by two-cell-tall trio of distinctive metal lakeside patio
   chairs in cardinal red, warm yellow, and lake blue, all facing north.
3. A six-cell-wide by five-cell-tall small locked boathouse with cardinal-red
   siding, a gray metal roof, flower and shrub skirt, and a centered dark
   south-facing door with a visible padlock.

Bottom row:

4. A five-cell-wide by four-cell-tall fire circle with a stone fire ring, cold
   embers, and four rough log seats arranged around it while leaving a clear
   approach from the east.
5. A three-cell-wide by two-cell-tall sturdy trail sign and small route marker
   with no words or symbols.
6. A four-cell-wide by two-cell-tall shoreline detail cluster with reeds, pale
   limestone rocks, driftwood, and a small patch of sand.

Use spring grass greens, lake blues, pale limestone, weathered wood browns, and
cardinal red only on identity accents. Warm yellow and lake blue belong on the
chairs. Do not include Pokemon, Nintendo, university logos, characters,
readable text, watermarks, or cast shadows outside the declared footprints.
Keep `#ff00ff` out of every object. Every silhouette must read cleanly after
reduction to a 16px logical grid.

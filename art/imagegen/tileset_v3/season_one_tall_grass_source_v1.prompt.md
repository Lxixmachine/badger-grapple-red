# Season One Tall Grass Source V1

Create one original standalone top-down tall-grass encounter tile asset for a late-1990s/early-2000s 16-bit handheld RPG. Match the project's hard-edged pixel-art language, dark green tinted outlines, and controlled cel-shaded foliage palette without copying an existing game asset.

- One dense square clump of upright meadow grass blades.
- Directly overhead / three-quarter top-down view.
- Readable after reduction to a 16x16 logical tile.
- Strict three-shade green ramp plus one dark green outline shade.
- No pale, white, cream, yellow, or neon highlights.
- No horizontal stripe bands, antialiasing, blur, lettering, grid, frame, or scenery.
- Adjacent copies should connect naturally along the left and right edges.
- Flat solid `#ff00ff` chroma-key background.

Reference: `season_one_vegetation_source_v2.png` for project-specific edge and foliage discipline only.

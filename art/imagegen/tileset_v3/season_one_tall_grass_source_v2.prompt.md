# Season One Tall Grass Source V2

Use case: stylized-concept

Asset type: production source for a grid-native outdoor encounter tile in a
16-bit handheld-style wrestling RPG.

The V1 source reduced into a dense dark square. Rebuild it as one low, wide
meadow-grass tuft with seven to ten separated blades, a dense lower third, and
open notches on both sides. It must remain readable after reduction to a 16x16
logical tile and must not form a rectangular carpet when repeated.

- Deliberately placed hard-edged pixel art; no painterly micro-detail.
- Three medium green cel shades plus one dark green tinted outline.
- Brightness stays near the pale mint lawn rather than neon or yellow-green.
- Orthographic three-quarter top-down view.
- One centered isolated asset on uniform `#ff00ff` chroma key.
- No scenery, text, frame, grid, shadow, duplicate asset, blur, or watermark.

Reference: V1 for project foliage language only, not silhouette or density.

# Camp Randall compact stadium source

Generated with the built-in ChatGPT image generation tool on 2026-07-17.

Purpose: an original stadium facade source for an exact 11x5 gameplay-cell
landmark. The production compiler removes the chroma key, fits the facade to a
176x80 logical image, limits it to 24 opaque colors, and exports it at exact 2x
nearest-neighbor scale to 352x160 pixels.

Prompt constraints:

- Original GBA-era collegiate stadium facade, not copied from another game.
- Cardinal brick and panels, cream limestone tower, charcoal recesses, restrained gold trim.
- One centered one-cell doorway on the bottom baseline.
- Whole isolated silhouette on flat `#00ff00`; no ground, people, text, logos, shadows, or watermark.
- Hard pixel clusters, limited ramps, no painterly noise or antialiasing.

# Camp Randall terrain source

Built-in ChatGPT image generation. Original 16px-grid GBA-era terrain family
on flat `#ff00ff` chroma: quiet lawn, limestone plaza, cardinal brick path,
interior wood, sacred competition mat, authored horizontal and vertical tree
borders, tree clump, hedge and flower beds, and ground transition pieces.
Ground is quiet; objects carry contrast. No repeated single-tree wall, logos,
official marks, or Nintendo assets.

## Prompt

Create an original modular vegetation and outdoor-prop source sheet for a
32px-grid top-down handheld RPG. Arrange isolated assets in disciplined rows
with generous gutters: two distinct natural 2x3 deciduous shade trees, one
1x3 natural conifer, one young ornamental tree, stump, single boulder,
three-rock cluster, round shrub, flowering shrub, hedge horizontal/vertical,
four hedge corners and endcaps, white/red/yellow flower clusters, tall grass,
reeds, horizontal/vertical wrought-iron fence, fence corners, open gate,
campus lamppost, wooden bench, blank signboard, cardinal banner pole, trash
can, bike rack, and bollard.

Use original crisp early-2000s GBA-era pixel art in a top-down three-quarter
RPG projection, readable at phone scale, with hard pixel edges and consistent
top-left light. Use natural Wisconsin summer greens, warm bark, cream stone,
cardinal red, and restrained gold. Large trees must show complete canopies and
trunks, never a forest wall.

Place everything on a perfectly flat uniform `#ff00ff` chroma-key background.
No labels, frames, overlaps, words, logos, Pokemon content, watermark, ground
plane, external cast shadow, or magenta inside an asset.

The raw built-in result is preserved as `_chroma_`; `_alpha_` was produced by
the standard imagegen chroma-key removal helper using border sampling, soft
matte, thresholds 12/220, and despill.
